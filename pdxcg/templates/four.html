Community

Students of PDX Code Guild are members of a vibrant extended community. The Guild features a large open community space that is open for graduates and other members of the PDX Code Guild extended community during most operating hours.

Students and graduates are welcome to come in and work on their projects and contribute to the community through peer mentoring.

Events are held. Programmer User Groups are hosted.

PDX Code Guild partners with PSU Business Accelerator, Portland Startup Weekend and Technology Association of America.
