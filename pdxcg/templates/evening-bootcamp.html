What can I expect at the Evening Junior Developer Bootcamp, and how will that help me become a good Junior developer?

You will learn core python, how to think like a programmer, and important developer practices like source control, testing, and debugging. Individual and group work, as well as personal portfolio projects help round you out as a developer. We'll introduce you to industry leaders and help prepare you to get a better job by helping you build your portfolio and helping you with your resume and interviewing skills.
