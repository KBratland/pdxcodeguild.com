Ryan Perkins

Student Bio:
I left Portland at the age of 18 seeking knowledge. This took me to Chicago, Philadelphia, India, Pakistan, Toronto and Oxford before Portland called me back. In the course of my journeys I picked up a Ph.D. and was a postdoctoral fellow at The University of Chicago and the University of Oxford. I am passionate about education, technology and innovation.

Student Goals:
A few years ago I had an idea for a startup. The idea was to use the transportation resources we have to get cars off the roads, build community and transform the ways in which we view transport. Practically this meant creating an app that would connect drivers with riders in real time who were heading in the same direction or starting from similar points. Because I was soon to move out of Chicago to England I wasn't in a position to pursue my idea until moving back to Portland in the summer of 2014. I quickly realized that if I wanted to make my dream a reality I would need to learn to code. I found PDX Code Guild and enrolled in the Python developer course. My goal is to learn enough to recruit the right team and create a prototype for my new business, PoolRyde.
